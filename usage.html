Usage Guide for Pi-Detector

This listening device embodies a raspberry pi which automatically detects drones through radio signal. You may have heard of the numerous complication in dealing with the raspberry pi machine - don't worry! We've sorted this out for you.

Step 1: Plug it in

Plug this miniature listening device in a stable outlet, Within 100 feet of the activity that you intend to monitor. Notice that the red light in the case will light up.

Step 2: Listen

You're done! This device will automatically start listening for radio signals in the general vincinity, there's nothing you need to do - feel free to leave it alone for hours, or days! Please make sure however, that the device is connected to power at all times.

Step 3: Collect Data

Plug a flash drive into the device (any flash drive will work). And WAIT for 1 minute, your device will be hard at work during the time to process your data and generate a report. After 1 minute, unplug the flash drive - This also power-off the device. The report will be in your flash drive.

Step 4: Pack up

Unplug your listening device and head home with your newly collected drone data!
